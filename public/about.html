Our Journey

From Stockholm to the Emirates

Learn about our journey, our values, and the team that makes us the UAE's most trusted moving partner

The Beginning

Kind Movers was born from a simple idea: to make moving a seamless and positive experience. Founded in Stockholm, Sweden, as ZeroMax Movers in 2010, we built our reputation on the Scandinavian principles of efficiency, precision, and customer care.

Our Growth

After a decade of success in Sweden, we expanded to the UAE, blending our Swedish expertise with deep local knowledge. This fusion allows us to offer world-class service perfectly adapted to clients in Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and beyond.

Today & Future

Today, we're the UAE's most trusted moving partner, having completed over 5,000 moves. We continue to innovate, adding new services and expanding our reach while maintaining our core values of trust and excellence.
