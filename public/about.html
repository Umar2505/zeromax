From Stockholm to the Emirates: Our Story

Learn about our journey, our values, and the team that makes us the UAE's most trusted moving partner.

The Beginning

Kind Movers was born from a simple idea: to make moving a seamless and positive experience. Founded in Stockholm, Sweden, in 2010, we built our reputation on the Scandinavian principles of efficiency, precision, and customer care. We saw a gap in the moving industry for a company that truly puts the customer first, and we've been filling that gap ever since.

Our Growth

After a decade of success in Sweden, we saw an opportunity to bring our unique approach to the dynamic market of the United Arab Emirates. Since expanding to the UAE, we have blended our Swedish expertise with a deep understanding of the local culture and logistics. This fusion allows us to offer an unparalleled moving service that is both world-class in its standards and perfectly adapted to the needs of our clients in cities like Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and beyond.
