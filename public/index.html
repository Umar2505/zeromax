Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about moving in the UAE

How far in advance should I book my move?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance, especially during peak moving seasons (summer months and beginning/end of school terms). However, we can often accommodate last-minute moves depending on availability.

What items are prohibited from being moved?

For safety and legal reasons, we cannot transport hazardous materials including flammable liquids, compressed gases, firearms, ammunition, perishable food items, plants, and pets. We also recommend you carry valuable personal items like jewelry and important documents yourself.

Do you provide packing materials?

Yes, we offer high-quality packing materials including boxes in various sizes, bubble wrap, packing paper, mattress covers, and furniture pads. These can be included in your package or purchased separately. Our premium and deluxe packages include packing materials.

How are fragile items handled?

Our team is trained in special techniques for packing and transporting fragile items. We use extra padding, custom crating when needed, and clearly mark fragile boxes. Glass, artwork, and electronics are given special attention. You can also request our premium packing service for valuable items.

What happens if something gets damaged during the move?

While we take every precaution to prevent damage, in the rare case that something occurs, we have comprehensive insurance coverage. Our team will document any damage immediately and guide you through the claims process. Our standard liability coverage is included, with options to upgrade for additional protection.
