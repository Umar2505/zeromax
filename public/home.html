Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about moving in the UAE

How far in advance should I book my move?

We recommend booking your move at least 2–3 weeks in advance to secure your preferred date. This is especially important during busy moving seasons when schedules fill up quickly. If needed, we also accommodate last-minute moves depending on availability.

What items are prohibited from being moved?

We cannot move hazardous materials such as flammable liquids, compressed gases, and chemicals. Items like firearms, ammunition, and perishables are also restricted for safety reasons. We recommend that personal valuables such as jewelry and documents be carried with you directly.

Do you provide packing materials?

Yes, we provide high-quality packing supplies including boxes, bubble wrap, and protective covers. Our materials are designed to keep your belongings safe during transport. You can choose between standard or premium packages depending on your needs.

How are fragile items handled?

Fragile items are carefully wrapped and cushioned using protective materials. They are placed in custom-labeled boxes so our team knows to handle them with extra care. For antiques and high-value pieces, we offer specialized handling options.

What if something gets damaged?

We take great care to avoid damages, but in rare cases, our insurance coverage provides protection. Any issue is documented immediately and our team assists you with claims. You can also opt for extended coverage for high-value items.

Do you move office equipment?

Yes, we handle the relocation of office furniture, electronics, and sensitive equipment. Our team ensures minimal downtime so your business can resume quickly. Specialized tools and methods are used for heavy or delicate machinery.

Do you offer cleaning services?

Yes, we provide thorough cleaning services before and after your move. Our cleaning team ensures that both your old and new spaces are spotless. This service helps save you time and ensures a smooth handover.

Do you provide storage solutions?

Yes, we offer secure short-term and long-term storage solutions. Our facilities are monitored 24/7 and equipped with climate control. This ensures your belongings remain safe and protected for as long as needed.
