Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about moving in the UAE

How far in advance should I book my move?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance, especially during peak moving seasons. Last-minute moves may be possible depending on availability.

What items are prohibited from being moved?

We cannot transport hazardous materials like flammable liquids, compressed gases, firearms, ammunition, perishable food items, plants, and pets. Valuables like jewelry and documents should be carried personally.

Do you provide packing materials?

Yes, we offer boxes, bubble wrap, packing paper, mattress covers, and furniture pads. Premium packages include all necessary packing materials.

How are fragile items handled?

Fragile items receive extra padding, custom crating if needed, and special labeling. Premium services are available for high-value or delicate items.

What happens if something gets damaged during the move?

We have comprehensive insurance coverage. Any damage is documented immediately and claims are guided by our team. Standard coverage is included; upgrades are available.

Can you move large office equipment?

Yes, we have the expertise and equipment to move office furniture, servers, copiers, and other heavy machinery safely and efficiently.

Do you offer international moving services?

Currently, we focus on local and inter-city moves within the UAE. International moves may be considered case-by-case. Contact us for details.
